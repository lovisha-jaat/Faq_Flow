Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about FAQFlow AI

Learn how the platform helps organizations automate their FAQ support.

What is FAQFlow AI?

FAQFlow AI is a company-specific FAQ management and chatbot platform. Organizations can add approved questions and answers and use them to answer visitor queries.

Can different companies use the platform?

Yes. Every registered company receives its own account, FAQ collection, chatbot and query records.

Can I add the chatbot to my website?

Yes. The integration page provides a JavaScript code snippet that can be added to a company website.

What happens when an answer is unavailable?

The chatbot displays a support message and saves the question as unanswered. The company can later add the correct response to its FAQ knowledge base.
